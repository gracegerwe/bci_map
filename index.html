Invasive BCI Map

Hi friends! These are the key companies, labs, and people shaping our future with invasive brain-computer interfaces.

This interactive map is made up of elements. You can:

  • Focus by hovering or holding an element, where you'll see its direct connections
  • Click on the elements to get a bit more information on each one
  • Change the color, fonts, shapes, and other formatting to your liking at the Settings tab on the right
  • And more!

This is by no means a fully comprehensive summary of the space. If you feel that we've left a person, company, or lab out, feel free to tell us below.

We made this map because invasive BCIs are AWESOME and we want more people to get involved. Use this as a place to start!

Cheers :)
